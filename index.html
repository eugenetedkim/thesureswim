More Than a Clean Pool. A Peace of Mind Guarantee.

Eugene Kim, Founder & Certified Pool Operator

Parent First. Pool Expert Second.

Hello! I'm the owner of SureSwim Pool & Spa, but first and foremost, I'm a parent. When my toddler started swimming, I did what any concerned parent would do: I became a Certified Pool Operator (CPO) to learn everything I could about water safety.

What I discovered shocked me.

I learned that many pool services focus on making water look crystal clear, but they often overlook the invisible dangers: improper chemical balance that can cause skin and eye irritation, harmful chloramines that affect breathing, and chlorine-resistant pathogens that can make families sick.

I realized most pool owners have no idea these risks are lurking in their water. They trust that a clean-looking pool is a safe pool. I started SureSwim to change that.

Our Promise:

We don't just clean your pool. We use science-backed, CPO-certified methods to ensure your water is truly safe for your children, your family, and your friends. We safeguard your swim.

You can enjoy your pool with complete confidence, knowing that every chemical is perfectly balanced and every test is performed with your family's health as the top priority.

Thank you for considering SureSwim. I look forward to giving you the peace of mind you deserve.
